About Us

Madha Pitha Hospital is a trusted 40-bedded multispecialty hospital in Kovalam, Chennai, established in 2013 by Dr. G. Hemanathan. We are committed to delivering high-quality, affordable, and patient-centered healthcare services.

We provide comprehensive medical care across multiple specialties including Orthopaedics, Cardiology, Neurology, Paediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, and more. With modern infrastructure, advanced operation theatres, ICU facilities, and cutting-edge diagnostic services, we ensure complete healthcare under one roof.

Our focus is on accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and compassionate care, ensuring the best outcomes for patients and their families.

We specialize in delivering patient-centered care through advanced medical treatments combined with compassionate support and personalized attention.

  • Facilities:
    • 3 Modular Operation Theatres (Laminar Air Flow)
    • 4-bedded ICU with modern equipment
    • 24/7 Emergency & Trauma Care
    • Pharmacy & Diagnostic Services
    • Highly experienced doctors and nursing staff

Orthopaedics

Advanced bone, joint and trauma care.

Our Orthopaedics department provides 24/7 fracture and accident care with modern surgical support. We specialize in joint replacement, arthroscopy, and advanced spine treatments. Expert surgeons ensure faster recovery with minimal pain. Physiotherapy and rehabilitation help restore mobility and improve long-term outcomes.

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Comprehensive brain and nerve care.

We provide advanced care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders. Our team treats stroke, epilepsy, and neurological conditions with precision. Modern diagnostics like EEG and brain mapping ensure accurate results. Complex neurosurgeries are performed using advanced technology for better outcomes.

Cardiology

Complete heart care services.

Our Cardiology department offers comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for heart conditions. Services include ECG, Echo, TMT, angiography, and angioplasty. Emergency cardiac care is available 24/7 with ICU support. We focus on early detection and long-term heart health management.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Comprehensive women’s healthcare.

We provide complete care for women including pregnancy, delivery, and postnatal support. Our team manages high-risk pregnancies with advanced facilities. Infertility treatments and preventive screenings are also available. Modern labour rooms ensure safe and comfortable childbirth.

Paediatrics & Neonatology

Care for infants and children.

Our department offers specialized care for newborns, infants, and children. We provide vaccination, growth monitoring, and emergency pediatric services. The NICU is equipped to handle critical newborn conditions. Our focus is on healthy development and preventive care.

Pulmonology

Expert lung and respiratory care.

We diagnose and treat respiratory diseases such as asthma, COPD, and infections. Advanced tools like spirometry and bronchoscopy are used for accurate evaluation. Our team provides long-term care and pulmonary rehabilitation. We ensure improved breathing and lung health.

Gastroenterology

Comprehensive digestive care.

We treat diseases of the liver, stomach, and intestines with advanced methods. Services include endoscopy, colonoscopy, and GI surgeries. Our team ensures accurate diagnosis and effective treatment plans. We focus on improving digestive health and patient comfort.

Radiology

Advanced imaging and diagnostics.

Our Radiology department offers CT scans, MRI, ultrasound, and digital X-rays. Advanced imaging ensures accurate and timely diagnosis. Interventional procedures are also performed with precision. We support all departments with reliable diagnostic services.

Critical Care

24/7 intensive care support.

Our ICU provides continuous monitoring and care for critically ill patients. Equipped with ventilators and modern life-support systems. Managed by experienced doctors and trained staff round the clock. We ensure timely intervention and life-saving treatment.

General & Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive surgical care.

We offer advanced laparoscopic and general surgical procedures. These techniques reduce pain and ensure faster recovery. Our surgeons handle both elective and emergency surgeries. Modern operation theatres ensure safe and effective procedures.

Vascular Surgery

Treatment for blood vessel disorders.

We specialize in treating arterial and venous conditions effectively. Services include angioplasty, bypass, and varicose vein treatment. Advanced endovascular procedures ensure minimal invasion. Our goal is to improve circulation and patient health.

Paediatric Surgery

Surgical care for children.

We provide specialized surgical care for infants and children. Conditions like hernia, appendicitis, and congenital issues are treated. Our team ensures safe procedures with minimal discomfort. Child-friendly care supports faster recovery.

Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery

Restoring form and function.

This department offers cosmetic and reconstructive surgical solutions. We treat burns, trauma, and congenital deformities. Advanced techniques ensure natural and effective results. Our goal is to restore confidence and functionality.

ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

Advanced ENT care.

We provide treatment for ear, nose, and throat disorders. Services include sinus surgery, hearing care, and laser procedures. Modern equipment ensures accurate diagnosis. Our specialists handle both minor and complex conditions.

Nephrology

Comprehensive kidney care.

We provide expert treatment for kidney-related diseases and disorders. Services include dialysis, kidney stone treatment, and renal care. Our team focuses on long-term kidney health management. Advanced facilities ensure safe and effective care.

Urology

Advanced urinary care.

We treat urinary tract and reproductive system disorders effectively. Services include kidney stone removal and prostate care. Minimally invasive procedures ensure faster recovery. Personalized treatment plans improve patient outcomes.

Endocrinology

Hormone and metabolic care.

This department manages diabetes, thyroid, and hormonal disorders. We provide accurate diagnosis and long-term care plans. Lifestyle guidance supports better health outcomes. Our goal is to maintain hormonal balance and wellness.

Maxillofacial Surgery

Facial and jaw surgery.

We provide surgical care for facial injuries and jaw disorders. Advanced procedures restore both function and appearance. Our team handles trauma and reconstructive cases effectively. Patient safety and precision are our priorities.

Psychiatry

Mental health support.

We provide treatment for mental health and behavioral conditions. Services include counseling, therapy, and medication management. Our experts ensure confidential and compassionate care. We aim to improve emotional well-being and quality of life.

Dermatology

Skin, hair and cosmetic care.

We treat a wide range of skin and hair conditions effectively. Services include acne treatment, pigmentation care, and cosmetic procedures. Advanced techniques ensure safe results. Our goal is to improve skin health and appearance.

Emergency & Trauma Care

24/7 emergency services.

Our emergency unit is fully equipped to handle all critical situations. We provide immediate care for trauma and accidents. Skilled doctors and paramedics are available round the clock. Fast response ensures better patient outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and patient care.

What services are available at Madha Pitha Hospital?

Madha Pitha Hospital offers comprehensive healthcare services including emergency care, ICU support, maternity services, pediatric care, advanced diagnostics, and surgical treatments under one roof.

Is emergency service available 24/7?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency and trauma care with fully equipped facilities and experienced medical professionals available round the clock.

Do you provide maternity and delivery services?

Yes, we offer complete maternity care including antenatal care, painless delivery, high-risk pregnancy management, and postnatal care with modern facilities.

Is NICU available for newborn care?

Yes, our Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) is equipped with advanced technology and experienced specialists to care for premature and critically ill newborns.

Are pediatric services available?

Yes, we provide complete pediatric care including vaccinations, growth monitoring, and treatment for various childhood illnesses.

Do you have diagnostic and laboratory facilities?

Yes, we offer advanced diagnostic services such as CT scan, MRI, ultrasound, ECG, ECHO, X-ray, and comprehensive laboratory testing.

Is cashless treatment available?

Yes, we provide cashless hospitalization through tie-ups with major insurance companies and assist patients with claim processing.

Do you provide ambulance services?

Yes, our 24/7 ambulance services are equipped with life-saving equipment and trained paramedical staff for safe patient transport.

Can I book an appointment online?

Yes, you can book appointments online or contact our hospital directly for quick and convenient scheduling.

Do you offer health checkup packages?

Yes, we provide a variety of health checkup packages including executive, cardiac, women’s, men’s, and senior citizen health plans.
