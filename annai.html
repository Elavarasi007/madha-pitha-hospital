Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our paramedical courses and training.

What courses do you offer?

We offer diploma courses in Nursing, Medical Laboratory, Operation Theatre, Anaesthesia Technology, and Accident & Emergency care.

What is the duration of the course?

All our courses have a duration of 2 years, including 6 months of practical hospital training.

What is the eligibility to join?

Students who have completed Class 10 or Class 12 are eligible to apply for our paramedical courses.

Do you provide practical training?

Yes, we provide hands-on training in real hospital environments to give students practical exposure and experience.

Is placement support available?

Yes, we offer 100% placement support and job assistance after successful course completion.

Can I pay fees monthly?

Yes, we provide flexible monthly fee payment options for students.

Where is the training location?

Training is conducted at Matha Pitha Hospital, Kovalam, with real-time hospital exposure.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply by contacting us directly through phone or visiting our center for admission guidance.

Do you provide real hospital experience?

Yes, students get hands-on training and real-time experience in hospital settings as part of the course.

Are there limited seats available?

Yes, seats are limited to ensure quality training, so early admission is recommended.
