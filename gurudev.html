Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and patient care.

What services are available at Gurudev Speciality Hospital?

We provide multi-speciality healthcare services including Emergency Care, Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Dialysis, IVF, Radiology (MRI), General Medicine, General Surgery, and diagnostic services.

Is emergency service available 24/7?

Yes, our hospital offers 24/7 emergency care with trained medical staff and essential facilities to handle critical situations at any time.

Do you have cardiology and heart care services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive cardiac care including consultations, TMT testing, and advanced procedures through our Cath Lab.

Is dialysis available at your hospital?

Yes, we have a dedicated dialysis unit equipped with modern machines and supervised by experienced healthcare professionals.

Do you provide IVF and fertility treatments?

Yes, our IVF department offers advanced fertility treatments and personalized care for couples planning parenthood.

Are MRI and diagnostic services available?

Yes, we offer advanced diagnostic services including MRI scans, lab tests, and other investigations for accurate diagnosis.

Are specialist doctors available?

Yes, we have experienced specialists across multiple departments to provide expert consultation and treatment.

Do you provide inpatient room facilities?

Yes, we offer clean and comfortable single and shared rooms with continuous medical care for admitted patients.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling our hospital directly or visiting us in person for quick and easy scheduling.

Where is the hospital located?

Gurudev Speciality Hospital is located in Kovalam, Chengalpattu District, Tamil Nadu. Please check the contact section for full address details.
